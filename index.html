    2023 Concert Catalog

    The 1975

    The 1975 Still...at Their Very Best: October 31st, 2023

    This concert was The 1975's Halloween show. All of the band were dressed up as different characters, and the stage was set with spooky decorations.

    My favorite moment from the show was when Matty Healy, dressed as Patrick Bateman, recreated a scene from American Phsyco.

    Lovejoy

    Over the Pond Tour: May 28,2023

    Lovejoy is my favorite band, and this was their first tour in the US. My friends and I woke up at 4am to go campout all day for the show.

    We ended up at barricade after waiting for 13 hours, and it was my favorite concert I have been to.

    Lollapalooza Aftershow: August 2nd, 2023

    This concert was a Lollapalooza aftershow where we decided to go only the day before.

    We got to meet the bassist for the opener of the show, Junior Mesa.
